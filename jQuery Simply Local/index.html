Simply Local Prototype 10/2014

Created by Matthew Eastman

Simply Local is a membership-based local food Community designed to expand North Carolina's local Economy by increasing local food consumption.

This prototype shows the basic use of the simply local map and directory on a mobile device. Future prototypes will look into creating user profiles with a local food tracking system for user to track their local spending and earn local points.

The local point system is a way for members to gain a higher rank and unlock more discounts. Later prototypes will also focus on implementing an area for members to interact and share information on a public wall.

Stay tuned!
